What We Do

We empower individuals to make sustainable food choices. Here's how we do it:


Strengthen local communities with educational cooking experiences

CWC regularly organizes cooking classes with local food pantries in an effort to effect positive change within people's diets long term


Provide nutrition and health education to local communities

CWC provides learning opportunities in the classes we provide to explain the "why" of every ingredient that goes into our recipes to better inform our audience


Improving accessibility through weekly recipes and food samples

CWC strives to give people access to variety foods by providing samples of the many foods we make and distribute

Our Foundation

About Us

Cook With Communities was founded on the idea that everyone, no matter their socioeconomic status, should have access to healthy nutrition. As a result, our mission became to provide services and learning opportunities so everyone from all walks of life in local communities could get fed while learning how to make nutritious food with the least amount of reosurces needed.
